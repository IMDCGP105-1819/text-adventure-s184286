Mini-Competitions

This door from the Foyer leads into a long narrow hallway, with doors down each side. Each door has a sheet of paper taped to it, covered with hastily-written instructions. As near as you can tell, these rooms are used by people who have little better to do with their time, so they throw together quick little competitions based on silly off-the-wall topics. There doesn't appear to be any judging taking place, so the only goal seems to be just to write a game that fits the topic.
